Mindscribe

For my University Final Year report, I decided to perform a study into how Video Games can be used in therapy. Part of this study included developing my own Virtual Reality Video Game in Unreal Engine that could be used to help rehabilitate people who suffer from cognitive impairments, as a result of dementia for example.
Mindscribe is a VR Video Game that implements various skill games such as Sudoku, Snap, and Mathematic Questions. These skill games h ave been implemented to encourage and improve cognitive skills such as problem-solving and mathematics.
Each mini-game features its own unique scoring system, which is dependent on a range of features, such as the time it takes to complete a puzzle or the number of lives they have remaining. Each score is then sorted and displayed in a scoreboard allowing players to track their progress.
I also implemented an AI, which allows for the implementation of games such as Snap, as the player has something to play against. By including traditional games such as Snap, it creates a feeling of familiarity for users which could help jog their memory.
