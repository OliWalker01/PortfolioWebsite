DirectX and Real Time Graphics - Demo

As part of my second-year university module, Real-Time Graphics, I was tasked with performing a study into DirectX and C++ to generate a scene with various user interactions using the DirectX Libraries.
I chose to generate a model bedroom with the main feature of it being a Scalextric style race track and car with a helicopter hoovering it. The render also features various hotkeys allowing for the player to quickly switch between different camera angles to give a different perspective of the model, one of these angles for example, being attached to the Helicopter giving a TV broadcast feel perspective.
