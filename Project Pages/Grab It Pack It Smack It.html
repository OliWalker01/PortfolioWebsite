Game Jam - Grab It! Pack It! Smack It!

Group work has been a major part of my journey at University, I have been extremely lucky to work with multiple talented groups. One of the outcomes of one of these groups is 'Grab It! Pack It! Smack It!', which was created in my second-year game jam in collaboration with other students on the games development course, as well as members from both the Games Design and Game Art courses.
Grab It! Pack It! Smack It! was developed in a week as part of a second-year group project game jam, it was designed to be a fast-paced game similar to the Overcooked series by Team 17. The game takes place in a warehouse where a tyrant warehouse manager reins. You must collect the orders as quickly, but as carefully as you can to protect the company's reputation.
During this project, I took the role of lead programmer overseeing a small team of three programmers monitoring their progress and evenly distributing tasks among us. The main tasks that I undertook were the item damage system and the scoring system.
The item damage system takes into account how much an item has been damaged by the player in the packing process, such as how hard it hits a wall and damages accordingly. This damage system also affects the score the player receives, as well as how long it takes the player to pack the product.
